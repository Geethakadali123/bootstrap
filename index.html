Welcome to Beauty Spot



IT'S TIME TO VISIT BEAUTY SPOT
Looking for the best salon to help you make an unforgettable statement? From our stylists who follow the top trends in hair color and extensions to our creative makeup and nail technicians, the expert staff at Beauty Spot is here to bring out your individuality like never before.

WELCOME

The salon provides numerous beauty and hair services such as haircutting, styling, hair extensions, body waxing, massages and hair coloring and more. Beauty Spot also provides services for occasions like weddings and parties.

SERVICES

Beauty Spot offers a full-service luxury menu including cut, color, ombre and extensions; plus makeup, nail services, tanning, massage therapy and more.

PRETTY PRODUCT SELECTION

Not only is Beauty Spot the place to get your hair done, it's where you get the stuff to keep it done right. Stop in to stock up on the finest hair care products from Amika and Aquage to TIGI and Nioxin. We've got you covered.
